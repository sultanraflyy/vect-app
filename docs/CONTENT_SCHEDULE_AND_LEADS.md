# Weekly Content Schedule & Lead Generation

## Weekly Content Calendar

The schedule is designed to maximize engagement while staying under platform
spam detection thresholds. Content is staggered across the week.

### Schedule Overview

| Day | Channel | Time (UTC) | Method | Status |
|-----|---------|-----------|--------|--------|
| **Sunday** | All channels | 08:00 | Auto-generate | Automated |
| **Monday** | Distribution | 09:00 | Make.com triggers | Automated |
| **Tuesday** | LinkedIn | 13:00 | Buffer auto-post | Automated |
| **Tuesday** | Reddit | Flexible | Manual post | Manual |
| **Wednesday** | X Thread | 14:00 | Typefully auto-post | Automated |
| **Wednesday** | Community | Flexible | Manual post | Manual |
| **Thursday** | LinkedIn #2 | 13:00 | Buffer (if 2nd post) | Automated |
| **Friday** | Instagram | 16:00 | Buffer auto-post | Automated |
| **Friday** | Outreach | Flexible | Manual send | Manual |
| **Saturday** | — | — | Rest / review | — |

### Posting Frequency Limits (Anti-Spam)

| Platform | Max Posts/Week | Recommended | Reason |
|----------|--------------|-------------|--------|
| Reddit | 1 | 1 | Strict spam detection |
| LinkedIn | 3 | 2 | Algorithm rewards consistency |
| X/Twitter | 1 thread | 1 thread | Quality > quantity |
| Instagram | 2 | 1 | Algorithm rewards engagement |
| Communities | 3 | 3 | Spread across channels |
| Outreach | 10-15 | 10 | Deliverability protection |
| Blog | 1 | 1 | SEO consistency |

### Signal Amplification Strategy

**Posting Timing Logic:**
- LinkedIn: Tuesday/Thursday 09:00 AM EST (peak B2B engagement)
- X: Wednesday 10:00 AM EST (peak tech discussion)
- Instagram: Friday 12:00 PM EST (weekend content browsing)
- Reddit: Tuesday evening EST (post-work browsing)

**Staggered Content Release:**
Each week's content follows a narrative sequence:
1. Tuesday: LinkedIn sets the intellectual frame
2. Wednesday: X thread expands with contrarian depth
3. Friday: Instagram translates to visual narrative
4. Blog provides the SEO anchor throughout the week

**Cross-Platform Narrative Sequencing:**
All content in a given week is derived from the same core thesis,
ensuring narrative coherence across platforms.

**Reply Strategy (Manual, Not Automated):**
- LinkedIn: Reply to all comments within 4 hours of posting
- X: Quote-tweet interesting replies to extend the conversation
- Reddit: Engage in the first 2 hours (critical for algorithm)
- Communities: Follow up on threads within 24 hours

## Lead Generation Logic

### Lead Capture Sources

#### 1. Reddit Discussions
```
Signal: User comments on Vect-adjacent post with a specific pain point
Action: Note username → research their company → add to outreach list
Tool: Manual tracking in spreadsheet or Notion

Example signals:
- "We've been struggling with content verification at scale"
- "Our trust scores keep getting flagged by clients"
- "How do you handle provenance in your pipeline?"
```

#### 2. LinkedIn Replies
```
Signal: Decision maker comments on thought leadership post
Action: View profile → assess fit → send personalized DM
Tool: LinkedIn Sales Navigator (free features) + manual DM

Example signals:
- CTO or VP commenting with a related challenge
- Founder sharing the post with their own commentary
- Comment asking "how would you approach X?"
```

#### 3. X/Twitter Engagement
```
Signal: Relevant account likes, retweets, or quotes the thread
Action: Check profile → assess fit → follow + engage
Tool: Twitter analytics (free) + manual engagement

Example signals:
- Enterprise accounts engaging with provenance content
- Tech leaders saving or bookmarking the thread
- Quote tweets that add their own perspective
```

#### 4. Outreach Responses
```
Signal: Positive reply to cold email or LinkedIn DM
Action: Move to warm conversation → schedule intro call
Tool: Apollo.io / Instantly.ai for tracking

Example response tiers:
- Tier 1 (Hot): "This is exactly what we're dealing with"
- Tier 2 (Warm): "Interesting perspective, tell me more"
- Tier 3 (Cool): "We might look at this next quarter"
```

### Lead Qualification Framework

```
Score each lead on:

AUTHORITY (1-5):
  5 = C-suite / VP at target company
  3 = Director / Senior Manager
  1 = Individual contributor

NEED (1-5):
  5 = Expressed explicit pain point
  3 = Engaged with trust/provenance content
  1 = General interest

TIMING (1-5):
  5 = Active project / budget allocated
  3 = Planning for next quarter
  1 = Educational interest only

TOTAL SCORE: Authority + Need + Timing
  12-15: Priority outreach (same day)
  8-11:  Standard outreach (within 3 days)
  4-7:   Nurture list (content engagement)
```
